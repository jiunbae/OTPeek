Frequently asked questions

I forgot my master password. Can you reset it?

No — and that's by design. Your master password derives the encryption key and is never sent anywhere, so nobody (including us) can recover it. If you're still signed in on another device, export a backup there and restore with a new password.

How do I move my codes to a new phone?

Either enable iCloud Sync on both devices (Apple), or export an encrypted backup (Settings → Export Encrypted Backup), AirDrop/copy the .otpvault file to the new device, and open it with OTPeek.

Can I import from Google Authenticator or other apps?

Yes — scan the QR codes from Google Authenticator's "Transfer accounts" export (otpauth-migration links are supported), scan any standard otpauth:// QR, or add secrets manually.

Codes don't match / are rejected by the website

TOTP codes depend on the clock. Make sure your device time is set automatically; a drift of more than ~30 seconds produces wrong codes.

The widget shows "No accounts"

Open the app once after installing so the widget can read the shared vault, and check that the account you want is favorited (widgets prefer favorites first).

Is OTPeek really open source?

Yes — the entire app (Rust core, iOS/macOS, Windows) is at github.com/jiunbae/OTPeek. Audits and PRs welcome.
